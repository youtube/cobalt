OnePass Frequent Flyer Enrollment

Continental Airlines and the OnePass frequent flyer program provide the best combination of service and rewards for frequent travelers. With over 3,000 daily flights, Continental provides members with tremendous travel and mileage earning possibilities and benefits. Learn more about the OnePass program.

To enroll in the OnePass frequent flyer program, complete the form below and then click Enroll.

The OnePass enrollment form is intended to be completed by an adult 18 years of age or older.

Already a OnePass member? Get OnePass PIN Help or sign in.

Read our privacy policy

Name

Please provide the exact name that appears on your passport or driver's license.

Age

Please indicate the new member's age below. This information is for marketing purposes only. Read our privacy policy.

Contact Information

The address you provide below will become your primary Continental address. This is the address where you will receive OnePass information once you begin earning miles.

Address Type:

Enter either a Home Phone Number or a Business/Other Number below.

E-mail Address Information

E-mail Subscription Preferences:

Online Statement
Notification of when your latest OnePass activity statement is available online.
Frequency: Monthly
News & Offers
OnePass program news and special offers from Continental and our partners available exclusively to OnePass members.
Frequency: Twice monthly (Additional e-mails may be sent based on offer availability.)
continental.com Specials
Travel specials and vacation packages including last-minute weekend deals on airfare, hotel accommodations and car rentals.
Frequency: Weekly
TripNotes
Confirmation of your itinerary, plus links to helpful travel information and tools such as weather forecasts, online check-in, travel guides, passport requirements and more. Your OnePass number must be in your reservation for you to receive this e-mail.
Frequency: Before every trip (The e-mail is sent approximately three days prior to departure.)

Personal Identification Number (PIN)

Please provide a 4-digit PIN that you will use to redeem OnePass miles and sign in to your continental.com account.

Username and Password (optional)

You have the option to create your own easy to remember username or password to sign in to continental.com. The username can be used in place of your OnePass account number and a password can be used in place of your PIN.
Your username must be at least four characters in length and is not case sensitive.
Your password must be at least six characters in length and is case sensitive.

PIN/Password Reminder

If you forget your PIN or password, we will provide this reminder to access your account.

Security Question

If you forget your PIN, we will ask you for the answer to the security question you create to verify your identity.
Please use English characters only when entering your security question.

 Security question examples:
What is the last name of your favorite teacher?
What is the name of your childhood pet?
What is your favorite car?
