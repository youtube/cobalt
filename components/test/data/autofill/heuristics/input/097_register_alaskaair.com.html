Benefits

Sign up for a Mileage Plan™ & My Account profile and we'll start you out with 500 Bonus Miles, plus establish an online account for you.

As a member, you can:

  • Earn miles with over 55 partners in eight categories.
  • Earn and redeem miles for award travel on 12 major airline partners to over 700 destinations.
  • Book trips faster and more conveniently (we'll remember your details and preferences).
  • View any Discount Codes available to you with 'My Discount Codes.'
  • Keep track of your scheduled trips with 'My Trips.'
  • Store Gift/Bonus Travel Certificates in your 'My Wallet.'
