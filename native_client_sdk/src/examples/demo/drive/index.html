This example demonstrates reading from and writing to your Google Drive account. To run:

  1. Click the "Sign In" button below to authorize access to your account.
  2. Click the "Get File" button to read the file "hello nacl.txt" from your account.
    • If it exists, it will be modified.
    • If it doesn't exist, it will be created.
  3. The file will be downloaded, and the contents displayed below. Try looking on Google Drive to see the changes as well!
